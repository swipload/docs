Swipload 3.3.0 release notes

December 3, 2023

Shipmentsâ€‹

Featuresâ€‹

New pricing model for shipments: base price + expensesâ€‹

With this you as the shipper can set a base price for the whole shipment and the carrier may add surcharges after delivery. This is typically used when an agreement exists between you as the shipper and your carrier. #2969

Carriers vehicle reference on shipmentsâ€‹

As carrier you can specify your vehicle number/reference on assigned shipments. This is shown to the shipper and can be used for multiple properties, like display on Bill of Laden and reports. #2958

Add new freight order directly on shipmentâ€‹

In addition to merging and moving around existing freight orders on a shipment, you can now also create and add a new freight order to an existing shipment in Draft status. #2961

Fixesâ€‹

  • confusing scrolling when opening a shipment. #2992
  • attachments and pictures for freight orders shown on shipment for carrier. #3000

Freight ordersâ€‹

Featuresâ€‹

Consignee detailsâ€‹

In addition to delivery location you may now specify the final consignee, your end-customer, on a freight order. Customise shipment list columns to add the consignee column and filter shipments on consignee. The consignee is displayed on the POD. #2959

Load quantityâ€‹

You may now specify a total quantity, (i.e. number of boxes) in addition to weight, loading metre (LDM), volume, pallets and dimension on a freight order. #2901

Changesâ€‹

Simplify address input/updates (even simpler than before!)â€‹

Looking up a business name and location is super easy when creating a freight order, but modifying the address could be a challenge. We've now made it easy to manually change the pickup, delivery, and consignee address. #2262

Fixesâ€‹

  • error sending new freight order that happened when navigating back and forth. #2900
  • requirements not always shown on Draft freight order. #2913
  • POD status not always updated for drivers. #2947

Swipload 3.0.0 release notes

November 5, 2023

Featuresâ€‹

Shipmentsâ€‹

Create, communicate, negotiate and get one price for multiple freight orders using a single Shipment. Order a FTL or LTL by combining multiple freight orders. A shipment can have a single or multiple pickup points and a single or multiple delivery points, and enables individual PODs for different end customers on a single shipment.

Shipments support the same features as regular freight orders, like:

  • Request quote from one or many carriers
  • Send to carrier as fixed price shipment
  • Send to carrier as time & material pricing
  • Communicate with carriers and drivers on a shipment
  • Share to spot market for instant resolving of your shipment need
  • Agreements, with multiple terms for your current carrier(s)
  • Integrations with ERPs and TMSs

and much much more.

Photo documentationâ€‹

When signing for pickup and/or delivery you may now attach one or more photos documenting the pickup or delivery. Photos are visible for the shipper and can be presented to the end customer.

Deviation handlingâ€‹

If a deviation occurs during the delivery process, you may now report it immediately, i.e. when signing for pickup or delivery. Attach photos to deviations for adding additional context and documentation.
