## Shipments

### Features

#### Improved shipment billing reporting for carriers

For shipments with a billing type that allows for expenses to reported by carrier after delivery, new options for reporting expenses has been added. Typical expenses like fuel, toll, ferry and more can easily be added to the shipment. #3090

#### View price/kg on shipments

Given a final price on shipment, the shipper can now easily see the price per kg based on the total weight of all freight orders. #3093

## Freight orders

### Features

#### New optional freight order status: Ready

I minor addon giving major improvements when multiple colleagues need to collaborate on freight orders and preparing shipments for transport. #3053
